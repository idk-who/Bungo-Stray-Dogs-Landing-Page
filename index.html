Bungo Stray Dogs
文豪ストレイドッグス
Follow Atsushi Nakajima as he joins the Armed Detective Agency in battling the Port Mafia and other superhuman threats to public safety.
Characters
Atsushi Nakajima
Atsushi is the protagonist, a kind but insecure young man who was orphaned and mistreated. He joins the ADA and learns to trust himself.
Osamu Dazai
A highly intelligent but suicidal strategist with a mysterious past. He has a dark sense of humor and was once a member of the Port Mafia.
Doppo Kunikida
A strict, idealistic man who follows a rigid schedule. He often gets frustrated with Dazai's antics.
Ranpo Edogawa
A genius detective who solves crimes instantly but is lazy and childish.
Kyoka Izumi
A former assassin from the Port Mafia who struggles with guilt but finds a new family in ADA.
Yukichi Fukuzawa
The calm and wise president of the ADA, who treats his members like family.
Ryunosuke Akutagawa
A ruthless and aggressive fighter with a deep admiration for Dazai, yet struggles with feelings of inadequacy.
Chuuya Nakahara
Short-tempered but skilled, he often clashes with Dazai, his former partner.
Ogai Mori
The cunning leader of the Port Mafia who controls the organization with an iron fist.
Francis Fitzgerald
A wealthy and arrogant man determined to achieve his goals.
Lucy Montgomery
A lonely girl who finds comfort in her friends despite her initial hostility.
Fyodor Dostoevsky
A manipulative and mysterious villain aiming to eliminate ability users.
